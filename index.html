ABOUT US

Welcome to The Museum of Candy, where colossal candyfloss constructions meld with a tantalizing taffy twistedness. If you've ever dreamt of nibbling your way through Candyland or scoring a Golden Ticket, you've come to the right place.

EXPLORE

Explore our sprawling sanctuary of confectionery bliss and be transported to an imaginative wonderland with a one-of-a-kind full sensory experience. This interactive art installation celebrates the vibrant colors and flavors of your favorite sugarcoated delights across over a dozen environments, from flying unicorn pigs to a marshmallow tsunami.

JOIN US

Join our candy kingdom guiding guests through our sprawling sanctuary of confectionery bliss. Be one of the lucky Candy Ambassadors to join our magical candy wonderland. We are looking for hosts, actors, waiters, comedians, dancers, anyone who loves people, loves to perform, entertain crowds, party and loves candy.
